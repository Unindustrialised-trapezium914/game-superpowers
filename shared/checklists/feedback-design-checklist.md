# Feedback Design Checklist

## Input acknowledgment
- every primary input has an immediate acknowledgment
- invalid inputs have clear deny feedback

## Combat / action feel
- damage has visible and/or audible confirmation
- danger states are signaled before punishment lands
- reward events feel stronger than neutral events

## UI feel
- menu focus and button press feel distinct
- transitions communicate where the player is going
- important HUD states are readable at a glance

## Sound
- there is a coherent sound hierarchy
- repeated actions are not sonically exhausting
- success / failure / warning cues are distinguishable

## Accessibility sanity
- important feedback does not rely on color alone
- important state changes are perceivable with audio off
